Word Clouds Analysis

We used over 55,000 Tang poems, 260,000 Song poems and 21,000 Song Ci from the Internet and sorted them by author and selected all the authors whose total work is more than 3,000 words. We did word analysis for all the authors and make corresponding word cloud for each of the author, including 128 Tang poets, 541 Song poets and 118 Song ci authors.
